How to Make Crispy Buttermilk Chicken Tenders

Crispy Buttermilk Chicken Tenders

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs Raw Boneless Skinless Chicken Tenders
  • 2 Cups Buttermilk>
  • Spicy Chicken Seasoning Blend
  • Flour
  • 2 Eggs
  • Cooking Oil

Directions

  1. Soak the tenders in buttermilk for 12 hours
  2. Combine the flour and seasoning blend in a bowl.
  3. Cover the chicken tenders in egg
  4. Toss the tenders in the flour/seasoning mixture
  5. Cook in oil for 12 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
