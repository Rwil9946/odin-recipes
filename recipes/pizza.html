How to make a New York Style Pizza

A delicious New York Style Pepperoni Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • *optional* Toppings of your choice

Directions

  1. Toss the dough, streching it out as far as possible until a circle shape is formed.
  2. Using a large spoon or ladle, spread the sauce almost all the way to the outside edge of the dough, going in circles. Leave about 1 inch around the entire edge untouched by sauce for your crust.
  3. Spread the cheese evenly, making sure to cover the pizza entirely so no large patches of sauce show through.
  4. Add toppings, if you so choose.
  5. Place the pizza in the oven and set a timer for 8 minutes.
  6. At 6 minutes, turn the pizza in the oven 180 degrees, so the half that was in the back of the oven is now closest to the door.
  7. Remove the pizza and cut the pizza into eigths with 3 long diameter cuts.
  8. Enjoy!
