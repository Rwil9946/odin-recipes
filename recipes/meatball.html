How To Make Italian Meatballs

Delciious Italian Meatballs

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Beef
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 lb Pork
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 2 tablespoons parsley
  • salt and pepper
  1. Mix all items in a bowl
  2. Roll into a 2 in ball, and place on a sheet spaced out about 2 in from one another.
  3. Bake a for 24 min at 400 degrees
  4. Enjoy!
